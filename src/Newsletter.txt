Welcome to Typespeeder 1.0.0 BETAVERSION.
In this version our game is fully playable. For playing you need to contact an Admin for registration.
Feel free to contact our admin to invite some friends.
If you have any feedback that could improve the game we would very much appreciate that!

INFO ABOUT GAME:
When you play on HARD level you gain more points and earn more score and xp. For EASY game play you gain half as much.
Highest level is 25. Continue to play to earn score to get high up in the Scoreboard!
If your game generate less points than your last game you will lose xp.
Words and letters are generated randomly for each game.
